What is Defacement Detection?

Defacement detection for websites involves continuous monitoring and analysis to identify any unauthorized alterations or modifications made to a website's appearance, content, or functionality. It utilizes specialized tools and techniques to detect visual or structural changes that deviate from the original design and content, potentially indicating a security breach. By promptly identifying defacement, website owners can take immediate action to restore the site's integrity, investigate potential security threats, and fortify their defenses to prevent future unauthorized access or alterations.
